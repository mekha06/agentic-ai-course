# AI Fundamentals

## Introduction

Before learning Agentic AI, it is important to understand the technologies that led to its development.

Agentic AI is not a completely separate field. It is built on top of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Large Language Models.

---

# Evolution of AI

```text
Artificial Intelligence
        ↓
Machine Learning
        ↓
Deep Learning
        ↓
Large Language Models
        ↓
Agentic AI
```

---

# Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence refers to the development of systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Examples:

- Chess playing systems
- Recommendation engines
- Voice assistants
- Self-driving vehicles

Goal:

Enable machines to reason, learn, and make decisions.

---

# Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning is a subset of AI where systems learn patterns from data rather than following explicitly programmed rules.

Traditional Programming:

```python
if temperature > 30:
    print("Hot")
```

Machine Learning:

```text
Data
 ↓
Training
 ↓
Pattern Learning
 ↓
Prediction
```

Examples:

- Spam detection
- Fraud detection
- House price prediction

---

# Deep Learning (DL)

Deep Learning is a specialized branch of Machine Learning that uses neural networks with multiple layers.

Inspired by the human brain.

Applications:

- Image recognition
- Speech recognition
- Face detection
- Natural language processing

---

# Large Language Models (LLMs)

Large Language Models are deep learning models trained on massive amounts of text.

Examples:

- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini
- Llama

Their core objective is surprisingly simple:

Predict the next token.

Example:

Input:

```text
The capital of France is
```

Output:

```text
Paris
```

Repeated billions of times during training.

---

# Why LLMs Matter

LLMs provide:

- Language understanding
- Text generation
- Reasoning capabilities
- Knowledge representation

They form the "brain" of modern AI systems.

---

# Limitations of LLMs

LLMs alone cannot:

❌ Browse the internet

❌ Send emails

❌ Access databases

❌ Execute real-world actions

❌ Maintain persistent memory

These limitations gave rise to Agentic AI.

---

# Key Takeaways

- AI is the broad field.
- ML allows systems to learn from data.
- DL uses neural networks.
- LLMs understand and generate language.
- LLMs serve as the foundation for Agentic AI.
